About Tania Rei

I am a New York City-based videographer with over 8 years of experience in creating cinematic videos. My passion lies in capturing authentic moments and turning them into timeless memories through the power of visual storytelling.

My journey into videography began with a simple desire to document life's precious moments. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a profound passion and a thriving career. With a background in fine arts and cinematography from the New York Film Academy, I've developed a distinctive visual style that blends artistic composition with authentic storytelling.

I've had the privilege of filming over 200 weddings and events across New York and beyond, collaborating with couples and clients who trust me to document their most important occasions. Each project receives my complete dedication and attention to detail, from initial concept discussions to the final edit.

My approach combines documentary-style authenticity with cinematic techniques to create films that are both beautiful and emotionally resonant. I believe that the most powerful videos emerge when subjects feel comfortable being themselves, which is why I prioritize building rapport and trust with everyone I work with.

Each project is approached with creativity, passion, and attention to detail

When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy exploring new filming locations, attending film festivals, and staying current with the latest cinematography techniques. I'm committed to continuous learning and evolution, ensuring that my work always reflects the highest standards of contemporary videography.
